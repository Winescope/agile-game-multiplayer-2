How It Works:

  1. When you add players, they get assigned IDs (1, 2, 3, etc.)
  2. Each player ID corresponds to a specific color
  3. When a player is assigned to a ticket (primary or helper), they keep their color
  4. You can instantly see who's working on what by their consistent color
